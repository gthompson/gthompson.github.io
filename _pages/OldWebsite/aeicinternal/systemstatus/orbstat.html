ORB STATUS REPORTS

orbstat

The following table of links results from running "orbstat -s" on various orbs, grepping out various strings including:

  • *.BHZ: this is looking at what continuous waveform data is on the orb from broadband stations
  • *.EHZ: this is looking at what waveform data is on the orb from short period stations
  • *db* : this is looking at what database row packets are on the orb
  • *pf* : this is looking at what parameter file packets are on the orb
