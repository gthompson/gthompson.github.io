DATA

  • Observations for ShakeMaps are collected from more than 80 broadband and 25 strong motion stations throughout the state.

  • Ground motion grids are based on maximum peak ground accelerations and velocities of two horizontal components.

  • Currently, ShakeMaps are produced for events with magnitudes greater that M3.5 with at least 10 associated arrival picks and calculated MMI greater than 2.0 at the epicenter.
