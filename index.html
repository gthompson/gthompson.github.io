Dr. Glenn Thompson

I am a Research Assistant Professor studying volcanoes, using seismic and infrasound data, at the University of South Florida University of South Florida (USF) within the School of Geosciences.

I spent the first half of my career building real-time seismic monitoring operations at Geophysical Institute where I was affiliated with the Alaska Volcano Observatory and Alaska Earthquake Center, having previously led the Montserrat Volcano Observatory through a long-term volcanic crisis while seconded from the British Geological Survey.
